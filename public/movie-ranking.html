The Best Movies of 2023 According to Bapparaj

My top Three movies

1. John Wick: Chapter 4

John Wick uncovers a path to defeating The High Table. But before he can earn his
freedom, Wick must face off against a new enemy with powerful alliances across
the globe and forces that turn old friends into foes.

2. Dungeons & Dragons: Honour Among Thieves

A charming thief and a band of unlikely adventurers embark on an epic quest to retrieve
a lost relic, but things go dangerously awry when they run afoul of the wrong people.

3. The Super Mario Bros. Movie

The story of The Super Mario Bros. on their journey through the Mushroom Kingdom.
